About Aria Hemphill

Origin

Aria was born in Guatalajara, Mexico. She is a dual citizen and was raised in southern Oregon in the foothills of the Cascades. She is an avid ultra-trail runner. She has completed several runs longer than marathon length. This includes several 50K's, 100K's, and even a 100 miler. Her goal is to qualify for the Ultra-Trail du Mont-Blanc 100 mile trail race.



Her mother, Kristen Horton, is an artist, home designer, bird enthusiast, and fellow traveler.



Her father, Ralph Hemphill, is an eclectic Mexican sailor and baker who resides in the bay of Salulitas, Mexico nearby tourist destination Puerta Vallarta, where Aria has more family.



Her boyfriend, Nick Marucci, is a programmer, engineer, and avid rock climber.



And the rest of her family is scattered among the west coast from Oregon to Mexico. She often travels far and low with her primos to go surfing.
